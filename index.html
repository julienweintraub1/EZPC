üñ•Ô∏è What operating systems are supported?
EZPC is designed for Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). It may work on Windows 8.1, but this is not officially supported. Mac and Linux are not supported.
üí∞ Is EZPC really free?
Yes! EZPC is completely free and open source under the MIT License. No ads, no tracking, no premium version. The source code is available on GitHub for anyone to review, use, or modify.
üîí Does EZPC collect my data?
No. EZPC does not collect, store, or transmit any of your system information. Everything stays on your computer. The app works 100% offline.
‚öôÔ∏è Will EZPC automatically update my drivers?
No. EZPC never automatically installs or modifies anything. It only provides information and links to official manufacturer websites. You maintain full control over all updates.
üõ†Ô∏è Do I need to install .NET or other runtimes?
No! EZPC is a self-contained application. Everything you need is included in the installer. Just download and run.
üíª What hardware does EZPC support?
EZPC supports all modern Intel and AMD CPUs, NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel Arc GPUs, and major motherboard manufacturers (ASUS, MSI, Gigabyte, ASRock, and others).
